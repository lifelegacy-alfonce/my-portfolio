About Me

I'm a passionate digital literacy advocate, software developer, and community leader dedicated to bridging the digital divide and empowering underserved communities. As the Vice Chairperson at GONLINE AFRICA CBO, he leads initiatives focused on digital literacy, financial education, and life-skills training, equipping youth and marginalized groups with the tools to thrive in the digital economy. Alfonce also spearheads the LifeLegacy program, a transformative digital literacy initiative that has positively impacted numerous young people by providing hands-on training in web development and essential technology skills. As a Public Relations Officer, he plays a key role in outreach and engagement, ensuring that more individuals access life-changing opportunities. With a deep interest in Bitcoin and blockchain technology, Alfonce is actively involved in Bitcoin protocol development, organizing a 2-day Bitcoin bootcamp to onboard students into the ecosystem. His work in this space includes developing projects using the Bitcoin Lightning Network and experimenting with Bitcoin node interactions. Beyond technology, Alfonce is the founder and host of the Beyond Adversity Podcast, a platform dedicated to inspiring resilience and personal growth. Through powerful storytelling and expert insights, he provides support and motivation to individuals overcoming challenges in various aspects of life. Driven by a vision of inclusive digital empowerment, I continue to push boundaries by training on Google Apps, encryption, and software development, and he is currently working on launching an affordable coding-for-kids program focused on game development.
